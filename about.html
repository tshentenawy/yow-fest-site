ABOUT US

The vision behind the festival.

OUR STORY

YOWFEST aims to become one of Canada’s leading multicultural urban music festivals. We bring together artists and audiences from around the world while constantly supporting and developing local talent through the Rise Up ecosystem.

Founded by J.C Jean, the festival is presented by Rise Up Canada (non-profit) and produced by Set-A-Card Entertainment.
