YOWFEST PROHIBITED ITEMS LIST

  • Bags: To ensure safety and streamline entry, all bags are prohibited. Exceptions are made for small clutches, wallets, or fanny packs no larger than 4" x 6" x 1.5", as well as medically required bags or diaper bags no larger than 16" x 16" x 8".
  • Weapons of any kind: This includes, but is not limited to, firearms (real or replica), knives, pepper spray, brass knuckles, and any other items that could be used to cause harm.
  • Illegal substances & paraphernalia: All illegal narcotics, cannabis, and associated paraphernalia are strictly prohibited.
  • Large & obstructive items: Items that can obstruct views or crowd flow are not permitted. This includes folding chairs, large blankets, tents, umbrellas, signs, flags, or banners larger than 11” x 17”.
  • Auditory & visual disruptors: Items that disrupt performances or cause a disturbance are banned. This includes air horns, whistles, laser pointers, and drones.
  • Commercial & promotional items: Unauthorized flyers, stickers, posters, or promotional giveaways are prohibited.
  • Outside food or drinks: With the exception of medically necessary items, no outside food or drinks are allowed on the premises other than FOOD FROM FESTIVAL FOOD VENDORS.

MAP & ACCESSIBILITY

YOWFEST is committed to a safe and accessible environment for all fans. Dedicated viewing areas and wheelchair-accessible washrooms will be available on-site.
