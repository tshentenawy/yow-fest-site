Parking is limited. We highly encourage the use of rideshares (Uber/Lyft), taxis, and OC Transpo to arrive safely and on time.

DO'S & DON'TS

ALLOWED ✅

  • Small clear bags (under 12"x6"x12")
  • Empty reusable water bottles
  • Small, non-professional cameras
  • Sunscreen (lotion only)
  • Positive vibes

PROHIBITED ❌

  • Weapons of any kind
  • Outside food and beverages
  • Professional cameras or recording equipment
  • Umbrellas, chairs, tents
  • Illegal substances

MAP & ACCESSIBILITY

The festival map will be released closer to the date, detailing stage locations, VIP areas, vendors, and first aid.

YOWFEST is committed to a safe and accessible environment for all fans. Dedicated viewing areas and wheelchair-accessible washrooms will be available on-site.
