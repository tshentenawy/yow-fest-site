RISE UP SERIES

MORE THAN A FESTIVAL

We created Rise Up to give local artists across Canada a chance to perform and get noticed. The Rise Up series reflects the full vision of the festival, bringing together diverse sounds and communities through three key aspects:

  • Rise Up Urban (English artists)
  • Rise Up French
  • Rise Up International
